biography

jjonah is the side-project of 21-year-old Sydney-based electronic music producer Jonah Newton, aka Equator. The jjonah project acts as a place for Newton to express purely experimental tracks without the worry of stretching ideas out into full-length tracks. Music released through jjonah follows a theme of creating from memories and childhood.
