biography

Jonah Newton, better known as Equator, is a 21-year-old electronic music producer based out of Sydney, Australia. Equator has gained traction over the past year, releasing his track "Held in Stasis" on San Holo's imprint "bitbird", and dubstep artist Samplifire naming him runner-up in Disciple’s “Biggest Remix Competition of All Time”. From bright bass tracks to mellow chillout beats, Equator incorporates elements of various electronic genres, taking inspiration from artists such as Virtual Riot, Porter Robinson, Jaron and Flume.
